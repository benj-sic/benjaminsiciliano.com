LinkedIn Debugging Steps

  1. Clear LinkedIn Cache: LinkedIn caches social cards for a long time. Try sharing the URL in a private/incognito window.
  2. Use LinkedIn's Debug Tool: Visit LinkedIn Post Inspector and paste your URL to see how LinkedIn sees your page.
  3. Check Image Accessibility: The image should be publicly accessible without authentication.
  4. Verify HTTPS: LinkedIn requires HTTPS for social cards.
  5. Wait for Cache: LinkedIn can take up to 24 hours to update cached images.
